app description

The app lets you search for recipes and sort those recipes by how close they are to a music genre. Sorting is done by counting number of adjectives that are associated with both the recipe and the music genre.

To search for recipes, enter ingredients and/or dish name, and press search.

Select music genre to sort and the most matched recipe would by in the upper left.

Clicking "show words" will display the adjectives that matched recipe to the genre.
